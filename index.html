-- About Us --

Your Support Can help a lot

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in the community through education, healthcare, and environmental initiatives. Our mission is to empower individuals and create a better future for all.

Since our inception, we have worked with various communities to improve access to education, healthcare services, and sustainable living practices. Join us as we continue to make a difference.

Why Donate to Onbright Siksha Foundation?

An act of good deed lasts long. Not only does it give you a sense of being a change-maker, but it also helps underprivileged people improve their lives. Contributing to NGOs working for the cause of poor and deprived people is a great act of kindness. It’s emotionally fulfilling, and when you donate to a charity that does real on-ground work, you become a catalyst for diving change in the lives of people who are on the bottom rung in the social-economic ladder.
